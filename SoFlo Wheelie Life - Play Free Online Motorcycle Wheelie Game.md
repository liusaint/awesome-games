## How to Play SoFlo Wheelie Life

Master the art of motorcycle wheelies with this simple guide:

### Start Your Engine
Click 'Play Now' to launch the wheelie life scratch game. The game loads instantly in your browser - no download or installation required.

### Control Your Bike
Use D to throttle and A to brake to control your speed. Use WSER keys for tricks: S for knee trick, W for seat stand, E for hand drag, and R for no hands. Press F to change bikes and G to change helmets. The key is carefully balancing your throttle and performing tricks to maintain the wheelie as long as possible.

### Master Your Balance
The key is to keep your wheelie going continuously while navigating through the environment. The longer you hold the wheelie, the better your performance.

### Avoid Obstacles & Go Far
Navigate around cars, barriers, and other obstacles while keeping your wheelie going. The farther you ride on one wheel, the higher your score in the wheelie life scratch game!
