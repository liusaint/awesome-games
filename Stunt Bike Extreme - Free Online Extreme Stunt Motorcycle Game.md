# Stunt Bike Extreme

A thrilling riding game that lets you showcase your best motorcycle stunts! Are you ready to become the ultimate stunt biker?

[Play for Free](https://stuntbikeextreme.org/)

## Stunt Bike Extreme Game Features

### Multiple Environments
Cities, jungles, warehouses, junkyards... Explore diverse environments for a unique Stunt Bike Extreme experience

### Overcome Obstacles
Experience the thrill of Stunt Bike Extreme by overcoming obstacles in varied environments

### Shop System
Purchase helmets, character outfits, motorcycles, and more in the shop to create your unique Stunt Bike Extreme experience

### 3D Graphics
Enjoy a unique visual experience with stunning 3D effects

### Numerous Levels
Over 270 levels, each with different obstacles and challenges, plus bonus tracks for even more Stunt Bike Extreme excitement

### Various Motorcycles
Multiple motorcycles, each with different stunts and speeds, for a thrilling Stunt Bike Extreme experience

## How to Play Stunt Bike Extreme

1. Use the up and down arrow keys or pedal icons to move forward or stop the motorcycle
2. Use the left and right arrows or the icon in the bottom left corner to perform flips
