## What is Take Care of Shadow Milk?

Take Care of Shadow Milk is an interactive pet simulation game featuring Shadow Milk Cookie from Cookie Run Kingdom. Originally created on Scratch platform, this game lets you take care of the mischievous puppet character through feeding, nurturing, and various interactive activities. The game has gained popularity on TikTok and is well-known among Scratch game players.

- **Interactive Pet Care**: Feed Shadow Milk Cookie, manage his needs like hunger, energy, and cleanliness in different rooms.
- **Multiple Room Experience**: Explore different areas including main room, bedroom, bathroom, and kitchen with unique interactions.
- **Creative Freedom**: Do whatever you want to Shadow Milk Cookie - nurture him lovingly or experiment with different interactions.
